Selection (357.3)

Contains the items appearing on the SELECTION LISTS. A selection can be composed of several fields, hence can occupy several subcolumns. Only the text is stored here, not the MARKING SYMBOLS.

Global: ^IBE(357.3,

Domain: Non-Clinical
